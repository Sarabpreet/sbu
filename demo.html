When you ask an LLM a question, it doesn't just look for a keyword match. It critically analyzes the intent of your query. For example, if you ask, "What's the weather like in Paris today?", the LLM can infer that:

  • You are asking for a weather report.
  • You need to know the location ("Paris").
  • You need to know the time frame ("today").
